Validating Forms:

Form validation is important for frontend and backend development.

Some examples of frontend form validation are: Required fields and specified formats.

Required fields are important when we need crucial information like an email from the user.

Specifed formats are useful to help users create passwords that are safer to use and include certain amounts of characters and specific characters

There are special built in functions to help validate things like email.
